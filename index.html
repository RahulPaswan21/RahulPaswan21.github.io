About us

"CITY INFORMATION" is a comprehensive website that provides users with relevant information about schools, hospitals, and restaurants in their city. Designed as a college project, this website aims to simplify the process of finding important locations by providing detailed results based on the user's search criteria.
With a user-friendly interface and intuitive navigation, "CITY INFORMATION" is a one-stop-shop for those seeking to explore their city's amenities. Whether you're looking
for a nearby school for your children, a reputable hospital for medical care, or a great restaurant for a night out, "CITY INFORMATION" has got you covered

These are some of our picks

Calcutta Jain Temple, Badridas Temple Street


The temple celebrates Paryushan in Bhadrav month (between 16 August and 15 September) with great enthusiasm that attracts a large number of devotees. Photography is strictly prohibited inside the temple premises.

Princep Ghat, Kolkata


One of the best places to get a quick break from this humdrum, is Prinsep Ghat. Situated along the banks of the River Ganges, this is a mini-vacation from home without the hassle of travelling too far

ISKCON Siliguri, Sri Sri Radha Madhavsundara


Legend has it that the place where this ISKON temple is built was the Baikunthapur Forest where Lord Krishna went into hiding. Therefore this place holds special place for pilgrims in the state.

Nehru Museum of Science and Technology


Nehru Museum of Science and Technology, a tribute to India's first Prime Minister, is a marvel of innovation and learning. Its exhibits and interactive displays inspire curiosity and celebrate the country's scientific achievements, making it a must-visit destination for all who love science and technology

Victoria Memorial


Amidst the tranquil gardens and serene lakes, Stands the majestic Victoria Memorial, A timeless symbol of grace and elegance, A tribute to an era of grandeur and splendor.

Howrah Bridge


Howrah Bridge, a marvel of steel, Stands tall with majestic appeal, A symbol of Kolkata's glory, A link that connects a million stories.
